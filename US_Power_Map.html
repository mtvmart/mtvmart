US Electric Power Generation

Renewable Energy Sources: Biomass, Geothermal, and Solar

Andrew Martin

This map represents renewable energy power plants across the United States, with circle size representing capacity in megawatts (MW).

Principle - I used a combination of incorporating shapes to de-clutter the map, as well as using intuitive colors.

Changes - I changed geothermal to squares, and solar to triangles to really hammer home the differences between the sources. Also, I tried to use colors that made sense with green for biomass, orange-y/red for geothermal, and yellow for solar. On top of that, I added an extra filter to only show plants with an MW value above 75.

I think this map allows easy navigation between filters, with obvious visual differences between energy sources, and a streamlined function/legend combination.
